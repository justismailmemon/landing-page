O Blockchain and NFTs

Blockchain is a shared register of data blocks connected to each other that use encryption methods to secure them and allow third parts to access the blocks and eventually execute functions.
Its applications are based on the concept of trustless trust among independent participants: this is why these networks are generally referred as peer-to-peerplatforms with the absence of additional institutional players to approve any transaction and decision. The financial executions and product/service deliveries are governed by coded methodologies calledsmart-contracts, that play a huge role in this cited concept, ensuring secure and transparent transactions and exchange of desired (digital) assets.
NFTs are non-fungible tokens, minted implementations where each token element have peculiar features that makes them unique and non replicable.

O F - NFTs

F-NFT, also referred to as a fractionalized NFT, is an NFT that has been broken down into fractions to be sold individually.

O How it works

Step 1

Party A Transfers Assets to Party B

The initial owner of the high-value asset transfers it to us, with F-NFTs minted by our organization to represent that valuable asset. We'll have an agreement with the owner that includes the following information:

  • The Value of the asset
  • The Contracts period (minimum of one year)
  • Stipulations for the asset
  • Confirmation that assets are free of liability and litigation and are Protected from any losses that may occur between other parties

The exact number of minted F-NFTs available will depend primarily on the current market value of the specific asset.

Step 2

The Sale of F-NFTs Begins for Party C

The next step involves the sale of the F-NFTs to our stakers, otherwise known as Party C. The sale will happen on the Opensea platform to provide investors with a secure, safe experience. While a custom marketplace for Physical Digits is in the works, we'll maintain compatibility with Opensea. The price for an F-NFT will depend on several factors, including the daily rent, estimated usage days in a year, length of time on our platform, and availability of F-NFTs.

Step 3

Asset Renting Begins and All Parties Benefit

Now assets are available for rent and are regularly managed and insured by Party D, enabling everyone to begin making profit. Each time someone rents a high-value asset, the money spent renting gets distributed across the board in our Physical Digits ERC 20 token, Phygits (PHD) , with each party earning a specific percentage.

Step 4

Return of the Asset to Party A

Once the agreement period ends, the original owner will have their asset returned to them. It's beneficial to the asset owner because they earn from initial proceeds, the rental period, and the depreciated value at the end of the contract.
