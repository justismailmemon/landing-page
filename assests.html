Fractionalized Ownership is the practice of multiple investors having ownership over a specific asset, such as real estate, luxury cars, private jets and yatches. With our platform, investors can experience what it's like to stake high-value assets by renting them out instead of owning them, creating an innovative opportunity that gives everyone involved the chance to benefit. With a traditional NFT, an individual invests in the non-fungible token and takes ownership of that asset. However, with an F-NFT, the non-fungible token is divided into several different fractions so that more than one person can have a portion of ownership over the asset. There are several types of F-NFTs available as a fractional ownership alternative that are garnering plenty of attention within the world of cryptocurrency, including:

  • Super Cars
  • Yachts
  • Properties
  • Private Jets

While once considered off-limits to the average investor and only available to the incredibly wealthy, the availability of F-NFTs makes high-value assets moreaccessible to everyone.
